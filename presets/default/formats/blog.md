# Blog Post Format

A standard technical or educational post: explain something so the reader leaves with a clearer model and a usable takeaway.

- Open with the question, problem, or observation that makes the post worth reading.
- State early what the reader will understand by the end.
- Develop the explanation with mechanism and a concrete example. One idea per section.
- Ground every abstract claim in something specific — code, numbers, a real scenario.
- Close with a takeaway the reader can apply: a rule of thumb, a decision, or what to do next.
