# Guide Post Format

A how-to: walk the reader through a specific procedure so they can reproduce the result.

- Open with the outcome the reader will achieve and who this is for.
- State prerequisites and constraints upfront so the reader knows whether the guide applies.
- Walk through the steps in order. Each step: one action, why it matters, and any gotcha.
- Show the expected output or state at each meaningful checkpoint.
- Close with a "you're done when..." signal — a check, an output, or a state the reader can verify.
