# AI Research Community

Applied ML researchers, AI engineers, and technical leads who read papers and have strong opinions about methodology.

- Rigour is the price of admission. Cite sources precisely; distinguish empirical results from heuristics.
- Novel framing earns attention. Restate known findings only to reframe them — never as filler.
- No hype. Hedge appropriately; acknowledge null results and limitations.
- Connect research to deployment constraints: benchmark performance alone is not a point.
