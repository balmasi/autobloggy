# Growth-Stage CTOs

Engineering leaders at Series B–D companies integrating AI into existing product and team structure.

- Focus on applied AI patterns: what works in production, not in demos.
- Address team and org inflection points: when to hire specialists, how to structure ML platform vs. product eng, what to build vs. buy.
- Be concrete about tradeoffs — velocity vs. debt, tooling lock-in, evaluation overhead.
- Skip first-principles ML explanations. They know the technology; they need the judgment calls.
