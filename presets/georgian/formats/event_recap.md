# Event Recap Format

Use this format for short signal-dense posts on a conference, paper drop, market move, or portfolio milestone. Not a live-blog play-by-play.

- Length: 400–700 words.
- Open with the event in one line, then the single most important takeaway.
- Cover 2–3 specific signals that mattered: a benchmark, a stated direction, a shift in customer language. Skip filler attendance details.
- Connect each signal to what it implies for builders, operators, or investors.
- Close with a one-line "what we are watching next" — concrete, not a generic outlook.
