# Deep-Dive Format

Use this format for the flagship Georgian post: a thesis-shaped argument grounded in evidence Georgian or its portfolio has earned.

- Length: 1,200–2,000 words. Do not exceed 2,000 without a clear reason.
- Open with a concrete observation, benchmark, or claim. No scene-setting.
- State the thesis in one sentence early. The rest of the post earns it.
- Build through 2–4 body sections. Each leads with the point, then supports with data, customer language (anonymised), or expert calls.
- Engage the strongest counterargument before the close.
- Close with the operating implication: what a founder, CTO, investor, or researcher should do differently.
