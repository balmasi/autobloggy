# Research Citation

Does every claim backed by research or analysis include a proper citation with a linked source?
Return `pass` if no research or analysis is cited, or if all cited research includes a linked source (inline link, footnote with URL, or full citation with URL).
Return `fail` if the draft references research, data, analysis, case studies, or metrics without a linked source. Naming an organization without linking to the source does not count as proper citation.

Return only `pass` or `fail`, plus a short rationale.
